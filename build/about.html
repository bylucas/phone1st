With over 16 years in design and pre-press, I now freelance and specialise in what I like best, web design/development. I found WordPress and liked the versatility and community backing which allows for the creation of quick, powerful websites.

I start websites with a blank canvas, they are responsive and powered by the latest technology and are structurally SEO ready. If you have your own design/theme, that’s not a problem, I can integrate your design/theme into a website or brochure.

I mainly work with small start-up companies and Individuals, helping with WordPress installations or quick fixes. I can help and advise on integrating your web design into a WordPress site, general web design or WordPress advice.

I also design bespoke brochures, menus, invitations etc, for small companies, shops, and individuals. I can arrange the printing or supply artwork as PDF, ( I usually supply all artwork on completion of a project). 

I enjoy the outdoors, especially walking, and have managed a couple of youth hostels in the past which I really enjoyed, meeting different people from different countries.

Privacy Policy

Who we are

Our name Phone1st
Our website address is: https://phone1st.org

What personal data we collect and why we collect it

Comments

At present there are no comments on this website should this change, and we start to use comments this privacy policy will be updated to accommodate comments.

Media

Visitors to the website can download and extract any location data from images on the website.

Contact forms

We collect names and email addresses from a subscription form which saves the information to Netlify (website host). We will use your information to keep you informed of new articles or the latest news regarding the Phone1st website. We also collect names, email addresses, optional phone number and messages via our Contact Form. We will answer any messages to the best of our ability and may contact you via email or phone over your message. We also use Netlify to collect your contact form information. Your information will not be passed on to a third party. Netlify has its own Privacy Policy which can be viewed here

Cookies

We don’t use cookies but search engines, social media sites may store cookies about this site on your browser.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics. The Google Analytics Privacy Policy can be viewed here

Who we share your data with?

Phone1st does not share any personal data with anyone.

How long we retain your data

If you use the subscription form, your name and email address are retained indefinitely or for the life of the website. This is so we can keep you informed of new articles and news associated with the Phone1st website. Any data from the contact form will be retained for as long as is needed. When a enquiry has been delt with we will do our best to remove the data within a reasonable time.

What rights you have over your data

If you use the subscription or contact form and have left data (name, email address, phone number and message), you can request the data to be deleted by contacting the administrator of the Phone1st website. Contact h@bylucas.co.uk – your data will be deleted immediately, and you will receive no more article or news updates from Phone1st.

Where we send your data

We send your data to Netlify and Google Analytics. Your data is NOT used by a third party for marketing purposes.

Social Media

There are links on the Phone1st website to social media sites who have their own privacy policies. These sites include LinkedIn, Behance, Github, Facebook and Twitter. Click on the link to view their privacy policy.

Our contact information

If you have any concerns about your data or this privacy policy you can contact h@bylucas.co.uk.

Additional information

How we protect your data

Your data can be at risk to hackers. Do not send any sensitive information via the subscription or the contact form eg. Payment information or passwords.

What data breach procedures we have in place

We have no data breach procedures in place eg. no data encryption.

Additional Links

Any additional links to websites on Phone1st will have their own privacy policies which should be able to be viewed on their website.
